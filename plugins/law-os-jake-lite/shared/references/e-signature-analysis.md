# E-Signature Analysis

## Federal Framework (E-SIGN Act)

- Validate intent to sign.
- Confirm attribution and record integrity.
- Preserve consent to electronic records where required.

## State Framework (UETA / New Mexico)

- Electronic records and signatures are not denied legal effect solely for being electronic.
- Ensure parties agreed to conduct the transaction electronically.
- Maintain reliable retention for later reference.

## Practical Checklist

1. Identity/authentication evidence retained.
2. Signature timestamp and audit trail captured.
3. Final executed record stored in immutable form.
