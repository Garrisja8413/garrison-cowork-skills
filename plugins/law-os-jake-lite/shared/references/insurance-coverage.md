# Insurance Coverage Framework

## General Coverage Workflow

1. Identify all available policies and layers.
2. Confirm named insured, additional insured, and exclusions.
3. Map incident facts to trigger language.
4. Quantify available limits and offsets.

## Policy SSOT

For each policy maintain:

- Carrier and policy number
- Effective dates
- Coverage form and endorsements
- Limits (per person / per occurrence / aggregate)
- Key exclusions
- Notice and proof-of-loss obligations

## UM/UIM Suite (A-F)

A. Trigger verification and tortfeasor status
B. Stacking and anti-stacking analysis
C. Exhaustion requirements
D. Offset / credit analysis
E. Arbitration or suit prerequisites
F. Demand package checklist for UM/UIM carrier
