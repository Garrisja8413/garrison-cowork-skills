# Law OS Jake Lite

Litigation workflow plugin for Claude Cowork/Desktop. 40 pack-first skills covering the full lifecycle of civil litigation in NM state and federal practice.

## Skills

### Case Intake & Investigation
- **ikp** — Intake & Pre-Screening
- **crhr-intake** — Civil/Human Rights intake
- **pli** — Pre-Litigation Investigation

### Fact & Evidence Extraction
- **cfp** — Case Fact Pack
- **dpb** — Deposition Pack Builder
- **dvp** — Discovery Volume Pack
- **lpb** — Legal Precedent Pack
- **mpr** — Medical/Provider Records Pack
- **psr** — Police/Scene Report Pack
- **cal** — Chronological Activity Log
- **qvs** — Quote Verification System

### Discovery
- **oa-rog** — Objections & Answers to Interrogatories
- **oa-rfp** — Objections & Answers to Requests for Production
- **oa-rfa** — Objections & Answers to Requests for Admission
- **drr** — Discovery Response Review
- **drs** — Discovery Response Sufficiency
- **drp** — Discovery Response Patterns

### Depositions
- **depo-notice** — Deposition Notices
- **depo-outline** — Deposition Examination Outlines
- **depo-index** — Deposition Indexing & Cross-Reference
- **depo-impeach** — Impeachment Analysis
- **synapse** — Deposition Copilot

### Motions & Pleadings
- **complaint** — Complaint Drafting
- **msj** — Motion for Summary Judgment
- **msj-spj** — MSJ on Personal Jurisdiction
- **mtc** — Motion to Compel
- **mtd-response** — Response to Motion to Dismiss
- **r11** — Rule 11 Sanctions Motion
- **trial-brief** — Trial Brief / Pretrial Memo

### Demand & Settlement
- **demand** — Settlement Demand Letters
- **dro** — Damages Range & Offer Analysis
- **dc** — Damages Calculator
- **dpw** — Demand Package Writer
- **ocn** — Offer/Compromise/Negotiation Strategy
- **sdc** — Settlement Discovery Controller

### Case Strategy & Analysis
- **co** — Case Orchestrator
- **pdm** — Plaintiff Defense Matrix
- **pcm** — Plaintiff Case Map
- **inference-engine** — Legal Inference Engine
- **sga** — Statutory Grammar Analysis
- **adr** — ADR/Mediation Diagnostics

### Correspondence
- **letter-of-representation** — Letters of Representation
- **gfl** — General Firm Letters

## Usage

Describe what you need in plain language. Skills trigger automatically based on keywords.
